## Verdict
Plant-based memory foam vs luxury innerspring hybrid — two very different constructions, both earning 10/10 overall. The AS3 wins on motion isolation, warranty length (20 vs 15 years), value score (9 vs 7), and side sleeper pressure relief. The Saatva Classic wins on response time and trial period (365 nights). Both score 10/10 overall.

## Decision rule
Pick the AS3 if: you share a bed and motion isolation matters, you sleep on your side and need pressure relief, you want a longer warranty (20 years), or you prefer all-foam feel. Pick the Saatva Classic if: you want the longest trial period (365 nights), prefer a responsive innerspring feel, or want the lower entry price.
